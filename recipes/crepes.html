Crepes

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries

How to make

  1. Place flour, milk, water, eggs, melted butter, and salt into a blender; blend until smooth and set aside.
  2. Beat confectioners'
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Pour or scoop
    batter onto the griddle, using approximately 2 tablespoons for each crepe.
  4. Tip and rotate the pan to spread batter as thinly as possible.
  5. Flip over when batter is set and edges begin to brown.
  6. Fill each crepe with 1/4 cup sliced strawberries and 1/3 cup cream cheese filling.
